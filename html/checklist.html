Focus

Requrements:The War Within

To gain focus you can use focus lens on a weapon/warframe and after reaching max level with that weapon/warframe a portion(depending on the lens) can transform into focus, by finding Convergence Orbs during missions that multily that afinity gained by using equipeent with lens by 10X and giving 5000 focus instantly, by converting eidolon shards or by killing void enemies.

Must be noted that max daily focus increases with MR.
